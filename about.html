About Us

Our Online Donation System is a simple platform created to help people support genuine social causes in a transparent way. We focus on three major areas: Education for underprivileged children, Healthcare for patients who cannot afford treatment, and Emergency Relief for families affected by natural disasters such as floods. Every donation is recorded instantly in our system and reflected in the total progress bar, so you can clearly see how your contribution is moving us closer to our goals. This website is built for learning and demonstration purposes, but it follows the same structure and flow as a real donation portal to ensure that the experience feels trustworthy and professional.

What we do

We help people donate to important causes through a simple, transparent form.

Your donation instantly updates total progress on the website.

How it works

  • Select a campaign (from Campaigns page or Donate page selector).
  • Fill donor details and donation amount.
  • Total donation and progress update instantly.
